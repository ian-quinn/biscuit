Please direct to the ConfTool system for your participant registration: www.conftool.pro/bs2023

STEP-1 Choose your registration type. A photocopy of the student ID or bonafide letter is required for the student ticket. If you need to register with accompanying persons, leave your request in the comment box on STEP-2.

STEP-2 Choose the conference programs you want to attend. Unless specified otherwise, the full-pass ticket includes all conference programs detailed here.

STEP-3 Specify your payment details. Generally, we accept bank transfers and credit card payments via PayPal. If not possible, we offer a payment proxy service that accepts payment via Alipay or bank transfer in mainland China.

ConfTool has PayPal API wired in so you will get your registration status updated upon a successful payment via PayPal. For other methods, please allow us 7 days to process your payment then update your status in ConfTool.

Also on this page, comment with any requirements, for example, the detailed information you want to include in the invoice.

STEP-4 Review your payment details and the registration terms, then proceed to payment information.

STEP-5 Fulfill your personal information (if you like).
