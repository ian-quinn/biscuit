Dear authors, welcome to the last step before your presentation at BS2023. Please login to the ConfTool system at https://www.conftool.pro/bs2023/ and complete the final uploads.

After login, click the "Your Submissions" button on the landing page.

Then you will see "Final Upload" button appears in your accepted contributions. Click it.

In the page of Final Upload, you will be asked to sign the copyright that authorizes BS2023 to use the recordings of your presentation for publication and distribution. In additional, by uploading the presentation video and poster, you allow them to be exhibited in the online poster gallery for all registered attendees of BS2023. At the bottom you may see three slots for your final uploads. All files must be uploaded before August 31, 23:59 CST. Overwrite existing files if necessary. Here is the check list for the final uploads:

  • All authors - Upload the camera-ready PDF version of your paper/report, in the 1st slot.
  • Oral onsite - Upload the presentation slides, in the 2nd slot. Note that if local video or macro resources are linked in your slides, you should use relative paths and pack them in a folder and upload the ZIP file.
  • Oral online - Upload the presentation video (≤ 15 minutes, ≤ 230 MB), in the 3rd slot. MP4 format is recommended. However, you can upload ZIP file as well.
  • Poster - Upload the poster (in PDF, JPG or source file) in the 2nd slot, and the presentation video (≤ 5 minutes) in the 3rd slot.

That's it! Gook luck with your presentation!
