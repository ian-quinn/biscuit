Effective discussion

Besides keynote speeches and interactive sessions, the conference will arrange a series of seminars on several hot topics including case study presentation in the field during 3.5 days. Moreover, technical tours, software demos and training courses will be planned during or pre/post the conference.

Paperless

Conference4me application will be used to replace the traditional paper guidebooks, while participants can get all the conference information and program via Conference4me application on their mobile phones and tablets. The ConfTool will be used as the webbased software for abstract/paper submission and review.

Low carbon

Each registered participant will get a transit pass with 7 dollars credit. The transit pass can be used for both subways and buses in Shanghai. In addition, cycling is recommended for this conference, and shared bikes can be rented easily by mobile phone application.
