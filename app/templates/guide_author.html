ConfTool manages the abstract/paper submission of BS2023: www.conftool.pro/bs2023

Register New If not registered before, you need a new user account to submit any contributions to BS2023. At the registration page you can create a new user account for the ConfTool conference management system. All fields marked with an asterisk (*) must be filled in. You may leave a message in the User Comments box.

Your Submissions Here you can submit new contributions and manage your submitted contributions. The number of contributions is limited to 3 per author. Please select one theme as the category of your contributions. Then proceed.

Submission of a Contribution In this page, you only need to fill in the form with plain text as an abstract. All fields marked with an asterisk (*) must be filled in. The maximum 800-word abstract includes 4 parts: Aim and Approach / Scientific Innovation and Relevance / Preliminary Results and Conclusions / Main References. You may need to prepare the text in advance in case that the ConfTool dialog expires. You may append any remarks at the end of this form. For example, if you need the administrator to help you change the category of your contributions.

After confirmation, you will receive a successful message and an email delivered to your mailbox. You can revise the details of your contributions anytime before the deadline. If encountered with any difficulties, you may leave remarks or contact us: info@bs2023.org

That's it! Gook luck with your submission!
