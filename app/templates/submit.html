Research paper

The high quality research work is always the focus of our conference. After the abstract is accepted, you need to submit your manuscript by the format of the following templates, which will go through the double-blind review process. Some top-ranked paper (regardless of presentation types) will be transmitted to indexed journals for further publication after enrichment. The best conference paper will be awarded at the ending ceremony.

Manuscript template Word Manuscript template LaTeX

Project report

Such reports should aim at the best demonstration of building simulation in real-world projects. The authentic data comparing the simulation and the actual operation performance is always welcome. This kind of contribution does not require the submission of a full paper but only the submission of an extended abstract. Please use the extended abstract template below. We are expecting 1~2 tracks in discussions for such practitions in industry.

Report template Word

Workshop

Workshops are open for the promotion and education of simulation techniques, such as software demo, vendor-sponsored trainings, and workshops for students teaming up with specific project. Submissions will be approved on a first come first serve basis, provided that there is a clear link with building simulation. The accepted workshops will be listed in the workshop schedule, and organized as a special track. The workshops are self-financed (either paid by the workshop organizer or by a participation fee).

Submission

The buttons below will guide you to the testing systems: ConfTool and Oxford Abstract. However, all accounts and submissions will be erased at the end of the testing period. After registration to the system, you only need to submit a brief abstract of your contributions within 500 words, with title and key words. We kindly suggest that you append all information about the presenter and co-authors when submitting the abstract. The information will not be disclosed to the third party.

Check out the following bullets before continue:

  • Your contribution must lie within the scope of the conference theme.
  • You must accept the copyright conditions at the end of this page.
  • You don't need a template during the abstract submission period.
  • title within 20 words, abstract within 500 words, 5 key words
  • The deadline for abstract submission is midnight November 30, 2022 (GMT+8).

You will find a short tutorial here after the testing period. If there's any problem or uncertainty using the conference system, do not hesitate to contact us.
