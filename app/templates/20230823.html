BS2023 Shanghai, China

Program at a Glance

Hi everyone,

Welcome to join the BS2023 conference. The preparatory work of the conference is now actively underway. We are pleased to share with you the latest news of the conference.

The program glance for BS2023 is now available for viewing on the ConfTool via https://www.conftool.pro/bs2023/sessions.php. We are happy to announce that we now have 5 keynote speakers, 19 invited speakers, and 6 workshops to share insights on the latest and most cutting-edge research developments in their fields. During the conference, we have 260 onsite oral presentations, 110 poster presentations, and 164 online presentations, contributing to the latest research on building performance simulation from various topics.

We kindly request all participants to take a moment to review the program. If you come across any errors or find that your paper is missing from the program, please notify us by email to info@bs2023.org before August 25, 2023, 23:59, GMT+8. It is essential that we rectify any discrepancies or omissions promptly to ensure a seamless and accurate program.

At present, we have opened the uploading entry of presentation slides and videos. We kindly invite you to finish the upload following the guidance by August 31, 2023, 23:59, GMT+8.

(1)

For the physical oral presentation, please upload your slides to replace the 2nd file in the “Upload Final” unit. Please note that each oral presentation should not exceed 15min.

(2)

For the virtual session presentation, please upload your presentation video within 15 min (no exceeding 230MB) to the 3rd file unit.

(3)

For the poster presentation, please upload your poster to replace the 2nd file in the “Upload Final” unit and upload a short presentation video within 5 min (no exceeding 230MB) to the 3rd file unit. Please note that you need to bring the poster printed to the site yourself. All posters, no matter when the session is, can be put up as soon as you arrive at the venue. In the poster session, you belong to, we recommend that you appear next to the poster to facilitate communication.

PPT and poster templates are available on our website (https://bs2023.org/submit) for your reference.

For those planning to attend BS2023 but have not yet registered, we encourage you to register as soon as possible no later than August 25, 2023, 23:59, GMT+8. Please complete the registration process (https://bs2023.org/registration) and inform us of your attendance. Please note that all registration fees must be paid by this deadline.

We appreciate your continued support and participation in BS2023. Should you have any questions or require further assistance, please do not hesitate to reach out to us.
