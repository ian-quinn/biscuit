Xiang Zhou

Secretary General

Associate Professor. Vice Dean of HVAC institute, College of Mechanical Engineering, Tongji University, Shanghai, China.
Dr. Xiang Zhou got his Bachelor, Master and Ph.D. degree in Tsinghua University from 1998 to 2008. He is the deputy secretary general of Shanghai Society of Refrigeration (SSR). And he was the secretary and volunteer of International Building Performance Simulation Conference (BS2007) in 2007.
