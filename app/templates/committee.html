Yiqun Pan

Conference Chair

IBPSA co-Chair, IBPSA Fellow. Full professor in the School of Mechanical Engineering, Tongji University, China.
Prof. Yiqun Pan has spent around 20 years researching and teaching in the fields of building performance simulation, green building technologies and community energy planning.
She is the fellow of IBPSA. At IBPSA, she has served as Member of the Editorial Board of Journal of Building Performance Simulation since 2013 and Member of Education Committee since 2017. At IBPSA-China, she has been Member of Board of Directors since 2012, and also the co-chair. In 2012, she served as the chair of the 1st Asia conference of IBPSA (ASim2012) hosted at Shanghai, China and the Vice-President of the Chinese Society of HVAC Simulation Special Committee.
She is the author of five books as first/chief author and over 110 scientific journal papers, leading the development of a co-simulation model of occupant behavior, IAQ and building energy – subtask 2 of the kernel development of whole building performance simulation platform (China National KeyR&D Plan).

Da Yan

Conference Co-chair

IBPSA-China Chair, IBPSA Fellow. Tenured associate Professor in School of Architecture, Tsinghua University, China.
His research mainly focuses on energy efficiency of buildings including human energy-related behavior modeling and simulation in buildings, DeST research and development, building energy code and standards, and national building energy and policy study. Author of over 10 books and over 100 journal papers. Since 2000, he devoted his effort into the development of the building energy simulation tool DeST (Designer’s Simulation Tool); In 2007, he is the secretary-general of International Building Performance Simulation Conference (BS2007), Beijing; In 2008, he won second national prize for scientific and technological progress of China; He is the operating agent of IEA EBC Annex 66 with more than 100 participants from more than 20 countries; Since 2016, he is the president of Association of Simulation in Heating and air conditioning of China, the co-chair of ASHRAE MTG.OBB and the associate editor of International Journal of Building Simulation.

Yingxin Zhu

Scientific Chair

IBPSA Fellow. Full Professor in School of Architecture, Tsinghua University, China.
Prof. Yingxin Zhu has spent around 30 years researching and teaching in the fields of sustainable building design, indoor thermal comfort and subway environment control.
She is an Academy Fellow of ISIAQ (International Society of Indoor Air Quality and Climate), a Chinese representative of IBPSA Board and IBPSA fellow, the Chair of National Steering Committee for Education of Building Environment & Services Engineering, and the chair of IEA-EBC Annex 69. She is also the former chair of IBPSA-China.
She is currently the editorial board members of Building and Environment, Energy & Building, Journal of Asia Architecture and Building Engineering, Building Simulation: an International Journal, Journal of Building Performance Simulation and International Journal of Sustainable Building Technology and Urban Development. She was one of the leaders who established the Green Olympic Building Assessment System (GOBAS), which played a significant role in design and construction of the 2008 Olympic stadiums.

Xiang Zhou

Secretary General

Associate Professor. Vice Dean of HVAC institute, College of Mechanical Engineering, Tongji University, Shanghai, China.
Dr. Xiang Zhou got his Bachelor, Master and Ph.D. degree in Tsinghua University from 1998 to 2008. He is the deputy secretary general of Shanghai Society of Refrigeration (SSR). And he was the secretary and volunteer of International Building Performance Simulation Conference (BS2007) in 2007.
Dr. Xiang Zhou’s research interests focus on human thermal comfort and behavior, radiant air-conditioning system, GSHP (Ground source heat pump) system. He is project leader of several research projects, including National Natural Science Foundation of China (NSFC), Specialized Research Fund for the Doctoral Program of Higher Education, the China National Key R&D Program during the 13th Five-year Plan Period, etc. He also attended some international cooperation projects with IVL (Sweden), VTT (Finland), UC Berkeley (U.S.), etc. He has published about 20 articles and papers, compiled 2 books and 6 patents.
