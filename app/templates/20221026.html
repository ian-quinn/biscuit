Abstract deadline approaching

Dear colleague,

The organizers of the Building Simulation Conference 2023 (BS2023), taking place in Shanghai (China), would like to remind you that the abstract submission deadline is approaching fast. The system will be closed on October 29, 2022, 24:00 CST.

BS2023 accepts abstracts for innovative research, project reports, and workshop proposals of all kinds. You only need to fill in the submission form in the conference system ConfTool after registration. Please redirect to our website for more information.

    Submit Now!    

BS2023 will be a Hybrid conference joining participants onsite/online seamlessly. We kindly invite you to submit an abstract to notify your interest and make an impact! Feel free to contact us at info@bs2023.org if you have any questions.

- KEY DATES -
October 29, 2022 Abstract submission deadline
December 10, 2022 Abstract acceptance notification
January 1, 2023 Early registration open
March 1, 2023 Full paper submission deadline
May 31, 2023 Final notification of paper acceptance
