All research papers will be double-blinded peer-reviewed and published in the conference proceedings, indexed by Scopus and EI, regardless the presentation form (oral or poster).

Some papers with high-quality will be selected for submitting an extended version to Special Issues of high-indexed journals, such as Journal of Building Performance Simulation and Building Simulation.
