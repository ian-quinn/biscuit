BS2023 Shanghai, China

Extended Deadline
for Manuscript Submission

Dear colleagues,

We would like to express our sincere gratitude for the time and effort dedicated to it. Considering the delayed notification on abstract acceptance, the organizers of BS2023 decided to extend the submission deadline by one week in response to many requests for more time to work on the manuscript.

The updated manuscripts submission deadline is Monday, March 27, 2023, at 24:00 GMT+8. We hope that this extension will be helpful to you.

In addition, we are still open for Project Report submissions focusing on the successful application of simulation techniques in real-world projects. Authors without accepted abstracts are welcome to submit their work as well. Please note that the project report will not be published in the conference proceedings.

    Instruction for Manuscript Submission    

Registration for BS2023 will soon open in early April and the registration fee has been updated on our website. Please determine your attendance format according to your schedule.
