ConfTool manages the abstract/paper submission of BS2023: www.conftool.pro/bs2023

Register New If not registered before, you need a new user account to submit any contributions to BS2023. On the registration page, you can create a new user account for the ConfTool conference management system. All fields marked with an asterisk (*) must be filled in. You may leave a message in the User Comments box.

After registration, you will be directed to the main dashboard.

Your Submissions Here you can submit new contributions and manage your submitted contributions. The number of contributions is limited to 3 per author. Please select the type of your contribution (research paper or project report), then proceed. (If you have submitted your abstract in the wrong category, please contact us for a re-evaluation)

Submission of a Contribution All fields marked with an asterisk (*) must be filled in. Please paste your abstract part to the text box, as a quick look for reviewers. You may leave any remarks in the comment box, for example, the source data/code repository. However, you must not leave personal information that violates double-blind reviews. Other options:

  • - Theme/Category: Choose ONE theme most relevant.
  • - Presentation Mode: Note that online attendees CANNOT give any presentations, either oral or poster. However, all posters can be accessed in the online repository.
  • - Student Contribution: Only the contributions with this box checked will be considered in the Student's best paper evaluation.
  • - Physical Attendance: Notify us if you plan a travel to China. The online attendees can only access all sessions by live streaming, with NO presentation allowed.

On the second page, you will be asked to sign the copyright-terms and upload the manuscript. You may update your manuscript freely before the submission deadline.

After this, you will receive emails about your successful submission and the copyright confirmation. Contact us at info@bs2023.org if you have any difficulties or suggestions.

That's it! Gook luck with your submission!
